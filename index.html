What is Saydia?

Saydia is the official Jailbreak University portal for education, links, and trusted jailbreak-related sources. It exists to help users understand what is real, what is fake, and how to avoid malware and impersonation.

Jailbreak University intentionally avoids corporate verification. Trust is established through transparency, consistent domains, and known community sources.

REAL vs FAKE — How to Tell

REAL Jailbreak University

  • Uses the domain jailbreak-university.github.io
  • No company‑verified or enterprise profile
  • No MDM installation
  • No forced installs
  • Clear warnings and transparency

FAKE / MALICIOUS

  • Claims to be “company verified”
  • Uses enterprise or MDM profiles
  • Uses a different domain or URL shortener
  • Promises “guaranteed jailbreak”
  • Pressures you to install immediately

Important:
Jailbreak University will never switch to a verified organization profile. If you ever see one claiming to be JBU, it is not legitimate.
